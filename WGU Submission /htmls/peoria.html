Peoria City Information

Peoria is a city located along the Illinois River, with a population of about 113,000. Known for its deep historical roots and significance in the American industrial revolution, Peoria is home to Caterpillar Inc., one of the world’s largest manufacturers of construction and mining equipment. It serves as a regional hub for agriculture, healthcare, and education.

Fun Facts

  • Peoria is known as the birthplace of the Peoria-style pizza, which features a unique crust and toppings.
  • The city has a rich history as an industrial center, with Caterpillar Inc. being a key part of the city's economy.
  • Peoria is home to the Peoria Chiefs, a minor league baseball team affiliated with the St. Louis Cardinals.
  • Peoria has a thriving arts and culture scene, with the Peoria Riverfront Museum and numerous theaters.
