Urbana-Champaign City Information

Urbana-Champaign is a twin city located in Central Illinois, home to the University of Illinois. Known for its vibrant academic atmosphere and as a major hub for technology and research, the city has a population of around 214,000. The area is recognized for its rich cultural scene, growing economy, and the prestigious University of Illinois campus.

Fun Facts

  • Urbana-Champaign is home to the University of Illinois, one of the top public research universities in the U.S.
  • The city is known for its annual Urbana Sweetcorn Festival, celebrating local produce and agriculture.
  • The University of Illinois boasts one of the largest library collections in the world.
  • Urbana-Champaign has a strong tech scene, with many startups and innovations emerging from the university’s research programs.
